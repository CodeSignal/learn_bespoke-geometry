Overview

This page explains how to use the Geometry Plane: an interactive 2D coordinate plane where you can insert simple geometry objects (points, lines, rays, segments, angles, and circles) by selecting a tool and clicking on the plane.

Getting Started

To begin using the Geometry Plane:

  1. Select a geometry tool from the sidebar (Point, Line, Ray, Segment, Angle, or Circle).
  2. Click on the coordinate plane to place the object. Some tools require two or three clicks (see Key Features).
  3. Your work is saved automatically in the browser. Use Clear in the header to remove all objects and start over.

Key Features

Point

Click once on the plane to place a point at that location.

Line

Click two points on the plane. An infinite line through both points is drawn.

Ray

Click two points: the first is the endpoint (start) of the ray, the second defines the direction. The ray is drawn from the first point through the second and beyond.

Segment

Click two points to create a line segment between them.

Angle

Click three points in order: a point on the first arm, the vertex (corner) of the angle, then a point on the second arm. The angle at the vertex is drawn with a small arc.

Circle

Click once for the center, then click a second point on the circumference. The circle is drawn with that radius.

Workflow

Here's the typical workflow for using this application:

  1. Select a tool: Click one of the tool buttons in the sidebar. The active tool is highlighted.
  2. Place on the plane: Click on the coordinate plane the required number of times for that tool (1 for Point; 2 for Line, Ray, Segment, Circle; 3 for Angle).
  3. Add more: The same tool stays selected so you can add multiple objects of that type.
  4. Switch or clear: Choose another tool to draw something else, or click Clear to remove all objects.

Tips & Best Practices

  • For angles, click the first arm point, then the vertex, then the second arm point.
  • For circles, the second click sets the radius (distance from center to that point).
  • Your construction is saved in the browser and will reappear when you return to the page.

Shortcuts

  • ESC - Close help modal
  • Click on plane - Place point or define geometry (depending on tool and step)
  • Clear button - Remove all objects from the plane

Troubleshooting / FAQ

How do I cancel a multi-click tool (e.g. after one click for a segment)?

Switch to another tool (e.g. Point) and then back to the tool you want. The pending clicks are cleared when you change tools.

Why didn't my circle appear?

If the two clicks are the same (or very close), the radius is zero and the circle is not drawn. Click the center, then a different point for the radius.

Is my work automatically saved?

Yes. The app saves your geometry to your browser's local storage. It will be restored when you open the page again. Use Clear to remove everything and start fresh.
